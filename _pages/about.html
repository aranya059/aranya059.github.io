I am a PhD student in Computer Science at the University of Texas at San Antonio, advised by Dr. Kevin Desai in the Vision & Immersive Realities Lab.

My research focuses on vision-language models for clinical applications, medical image segmentation, and trustworthy AI for healthcare, with an emphasis on data-efficient learning to cut annotation costs. See my publications here.

Before my PhD, I spent 2.5 years as a software engineer at Nascenia Ltd and RedDot Digital Ltd. I received my B.Sc. in Computer Science and Engineering from KUET in 2020.
